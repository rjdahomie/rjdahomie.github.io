The P2 Prosthetic Hand

Team leader of a year-long prosthetic hand project for the PLTW Engineering Design and Development. Engineered a prosthetic hand from scratch efficient enough to our evaluations and standards to adequately support an individual with a hand disability. This project included 3D modeling using Autodesk Inventor, 3D printing, and Java coding. This project was awarded by the Orange County Uplift Foundation.
